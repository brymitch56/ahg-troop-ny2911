Welcome!

American Heritage Girls is a national Christ-centered character and leadership development program for girls ages 5 to 18. Girls earn badges, serve their communities, grow in their faith, and build friendships that last a lifetime. Troop NY2911 welcomes girls of all levels — Pathfinders, Tenderhearts, Explorers, Pioneers, and Patriots.

Faith

Girls grow in their relationship with God through a Christ-centered program built on Biblical values.

Service

From community service projects to national service stars, girls learn the joy of serving others.

Fun

Badge work, camping, outdoor adventures, special events, and friendships across all age levels.
